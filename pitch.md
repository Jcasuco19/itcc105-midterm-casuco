# ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot - Pitch

## Problem Statement
The old ASSCAT student portal relies heavily on a menu-based interface that is confusing, slow, and inefficient. Students must click through multiple pages just to complete simple tasks like checking clearance, schedules, or announcements. This results in wasted time, poor user experience, and frustration.

## Industry 5.0 Justification
The ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot empowers students by acting as an intelligent assistant rather than replacing human roles. It enhances human decision-making by providing instant answers, personalized guidance, and real-time support. Instead of removing staff roles, it reduces repetitive inquiries so staff can focus on more meaningful student engagement.

## Agile User Stories

1. As a student, I want to ask the AI about my clearance status, so that I can quickly know if I have pending requirements.

2. As a student, I want the AI to show my class schedule instantly, so that I don’t have to navigate multiple pages.

3. As a student, I want to receive announcements through the AI, so that I stay updated without checking different sections of the portal.
